Education

The Department of Physics was a great environment – we were a small group, which made it friendly and supportive, not only among students but also from the lecturers. During my studies, I had the opportunity to try many different things. Over time, I realized that what I enjoyed most of all was programming. And especially programming in LabVIEW. That is why I decided to dedicate my Master's thesis to this field. It was there that I first truly immersed myself in application logic – not just “writing some code,” but designing how an application should function as a whole. That was one of the key moments that gradually led me to pursue a career in software development.
